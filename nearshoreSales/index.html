DevOps Team

Handles deployments to production and technical support of tickets related to the product. This teams handles the first two tiers of support
Tier 1: Initial support level responsible for basic customer issues
Tier 2: Deployment and configuration issues

Enhancements Team

This team is specialized in detecting new opportunities for product enhancements that will add value to your software solution. The team has a different composition than the development team and uses fast prototyping techniques to deliver demos or proof of concept solutions where hypotheses about product enhancements can be proven and later developed by a team if proven valuable for your business.

Development Team

The development teams are shaped after initial requirements set in the discovery phase. One or several agile teams are created containing multidisciplinary roles. We ensure these teams are aligned with the business objectives.
